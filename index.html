About

My name is Sharon Plunkett, I'm an Irish Front End Developer living in Los Angeles, California with a full stack background & a degree in Computer Science. This portfolio is designed to showcase recent work that I have completed on the front end as I continue to explore the incredibly rich & colorful world of front end website development.

Past

I've always taken an interest in how things work & am especially keen on building things. Through belief in learning by doing, I have come to appreciate the importance of design & planning. For a while I was immersed in the server side of the technology stack. The model & the controller. Data, databases, servers & application logic.

On the server side I have written applications for data organisation, network connectivity & application logic. Scripting in perl & php. Coding in C, C++ & Java. Working within large organizations I have first hand experience of the many challenges of enterprise scale development, emphasis on security, reliability & robustness.

My introduction to the front end came by way of building working prototypes for web applications & services concerned with the View & the presentation of information. Since then elemental challenges such as user experence & interacvitity in building useful & beautiful portals have driven me to explore ever changing technologies, practices & techniques.

Present

It's work on the client side, presenting content in a meaningful & hopefully beautiful way that I endevor to work on these days. Crafting interfaces, presenting content & data with consideration for the user experience, responsively & accessibly, incorporating best design patterns & adhering to the principals of progressive enhancent while maximizing performance is a fantastic challenge that I am committed to mastering.

Furthermore, I write, surf, make furnitire, take photographs & edit them & appreciate great art & good music. As well as being a voracious reader, I'm a writer of short stories & fiction. I cultivate this often challenging habit with classes and a ubiquitous notebook. I'm a photographer, bringing my well used SLR nearly everywhere & try to document my surroundings with subjective objectivity! That every shot doesn't work out has made me a practiced Photoshop editor with layers & masks. One of the nicest things about living in a city as grand as Los Angeles is proximity to so many phenomenal art museums & galleries, which I frequent, camera in hand as often as possible.

These varied pursuits have cultivated my interest in & love for design, &enabled continued growth & progression in my design ability, aesthetic & ambition.
